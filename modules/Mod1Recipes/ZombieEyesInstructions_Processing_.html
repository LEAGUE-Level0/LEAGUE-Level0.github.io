Zombie Eyes

We are going to make a face with animated eyes. You could use your own face (take a picture with photobooth) or a zombie, a cat, or whatever you like. Whichever you choose, the eyes should be prominent.

1. Start a new sketch with setup() and draw() methods.

2. Drop the image of the face onto your sketch. Load it like this in the setup method:

PImage face = loadImage("face.jpeg");
image(face, 0, 0);

3. Adjust the size of your sketch if necessary.

size(width, height);

Or adjust the size of your image like this:

face.resize(width, height);

IMPORTANT: Make sure you use the size or resize command before the image command.

4. Place 2 ellipses over the irises of the eyes in the draw method.

ellipse(x, y, width, height);

5. Give the irises a color with the fill command (use numbers in place of red, green, blue).

fill(red, green, blue);

6. Change the color of the irises when the mouse moves.

mouseX
mouseY

7. Draw black pupils on top of the irises.

8. OPTIONAL: Have the pupils follow the mouse.
