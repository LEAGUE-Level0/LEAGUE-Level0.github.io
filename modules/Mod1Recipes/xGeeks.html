X-Geeks

Everyone has a superpower. Mine is writing recipes. This program will store the superpowers of all the people in this classroom. E.g. If I type my name ("Java Teacher"), your program should say "Java Teacher's superpower is writing recipes".

Ask your classmates for their superpowers and save the superpower for each person in a separate variable.

Ask the user to enter their name. Store their answer in a variable.

Depending on the name entered, show the correct superpower in a pop-up.
